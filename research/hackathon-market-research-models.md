# Market Research Models from Recent Hackathons (AI)

Research done for inspiration: how other hackathons approached competitive analysis and market research.

---

## 1. SalesShortcut (Grand Prize, Google ADK Hackathon 2025)

**Model: "Real Pain Point + Automation Gap"**

- Started from a problem observed in real life (friend making manual cold calls)
- Mapped the current manual process vs. what AI can automate
- Positioned as an "accessibility tool" for those without sales infrastructure
- 34 specialized agents, 5 microservices, A2A protocol

**Research structure:**
1. Real observed problem
2. Current manual process (how it works today)
3. Automation gap (what AI can solve)
4. Proposed solution
5. Startup launched after the hackathon (validation)

**Link:** https://devpost.com/software/salesshortcut

---

## 2. FoundryAI (Internet of Agents Hackathon, Lablab 2025)

**Model: "Market Research as a Multi-Agent Product"**

- Created agents that automatically generate: problem analysis, market research, competitor analysis, business model
- Output: complete pitch with market sizing and business model generated by AI

**Research structure (automated by agents):**
1. Researcher Agent: collects market data
2. Analyst Agent: identifies competitors and gaps
3. Pitch Agent: generates presentation with problem/solution/market/model
4. Design Agent: creates logo and visual materials

**Link:** https://lablab.ai/event/internet-of-agents/foundryai/foundryai-multi-agent-startup-pitch-generator

## 3. RiskWise (Best Overall $20k, Microsoft AI Agents Hackathon 2025)

**Model: "Real-Time Intelligence + Risk Matrix"**

- Market intelligence agent for supply chain
- Continuously monitors external sources (news, tariffs, geopolitics)
- Classifies risks by impact and probability
- User asks questions in natural language, receives visual insights

**Research structure:**
1. Real-time data sources (news, regulations, tariffs)
2. Continuous monitoring by agents
3. Risk classification (impact x probability)
4. Interactive visualization (dashboard)
5. Natural language Q&A on the data

**Stack:** Python, React/Next.js, Azure AI Agent Service, Semantic Kernel, SQL

**Link:** https://github.com/microsoft/AI_Agents_Hackathon/issues/526

## 4. MarketPulse Agent (Internet of Agents Hackathon, Lablab 2025)

**Model: "Competitive Intelligence Automation"**

- Automates competitive intelligence with AI agents
- Monitors competitors in real time
- Delivers automatic alerts and reports

**Research structure:**
1. Define competitors to monitor
2. Scraping/monitoring agents
3. Change analysis (new features, pricing, positioning)
4. Automatic alerts
5. Periodic reports

---

## 5. Venture313 AI Buildathon (Detroit, 2025)

**Model: "TAM/SAM/SOM + Competitive Positioning"**

- Required in the pitch: product description, target user, market opportunity, monetization
- Evaluated by: market potential, feasibility, go-to-market clarity, differentiation

**Research structure (required by the hackathon):**
1. TAM (total addressable market)
2. SAM (serviceable addressable market)
3. SOM (serviceable obtainable market)
4. Positioning vs. competitors
5. Clear differentiation
6. Monetization model

**Link:** https://v313detroitbuildathon2025.devpost.com/
