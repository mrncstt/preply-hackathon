# Presentation Script

> 5 minutes total: 1 min problem, 1 min solution, 3 min live demo
> Open https://botas.vercel.app/submission-pitch.html before starting

---

## VERSION A: Serious / Professional

### Slide 1: Title (15s)

"Quick show of hands. Who here has seen Breaking Bad? Ratatouille? Ted Lasso?

Cool. Do you know what these three have in common?

Walter White was a boring chemistry teacher until he applied chemistry to something he was passionate about. Remy was just a rat until he connected his obsession with cooking to a real kitchen. Ted Lasso knew nothing about football but connected with people through what he already knew.

They all took what they loved and used it to master what they needed. That's exactly what we built. For language learning."

*[click]*

### Slide 2: Problem (25s)

"73% of language learners quit within 3 months. That's not because learning is hard. It's because no one really understood what they care about. We all have that graveyard of abandoned learning attempts. The app streak that died. The online course we never finished."

*[click]*

### Slide 3: Before vs After (25s)

"Today on Preply, you fill a form, pick a tutor from a list, and the tutor starts from zero. With our agent, you have a 90-second voice conversation. The AI discovers your passions. And the tutor gets your full profile before lesson 1. No more blank slates."

*[click]*

### Slide 4: Solution (30s)

"Our solution is the AI Discovery Coach. It's a 90-second multilingual voice interview. It finds your passions on one side, your goals on the other, and creates what we call a Learning Bridge. For example, if you love cooking and football, the bridge might be: learn Spanish through recipe videos and match commentary. Built with Agora ConvoAI, OpenAI, and Thymia."

*[click]*

### Slide 5: Demo Flow (15s)

"Here's the full journey. Landing page, voice interview with Thymia cognitive signals, AI builds your profile, saves it to persistent memory, creates the Learning Bridge, finds matching tutors, and connects you to Preply. Let me show you."

*[click to botas.vercel.app]*

## Tips

- **Before presenting**: Open botas.vercel.app in a browser tab AND submission-pitch.html in another tab
- **WiFi backup**: If the demo fails, show the example on slide 6 instead
- **Timing**: Practice the demo conversation beforehand. Know what you'll say so it flows naturally
- **Mic check**: Make sure your laptop mic works with the voice agent before going on stage
- **Killer line**: "The tutor gets this profile before lesson 1. The first session is never a blank slate." Say it with conviction.
