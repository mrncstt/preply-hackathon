## Our differentiator

We are the only team that:
1. Replaces the Preply onboarding form entirely (not a between-lessons tool)
2. Creates a "Learning Bridge" connecting passions to language goals
3. Delivers a structured learner profile to tutors before lesson 1
4. Focuses on first-session retention (highest-leverage metric for a marketplace)

Most teams (Saturn, Loop, Crooked Horn, Gulp) focus on between/after lessons. We focus on before lesson 1.
